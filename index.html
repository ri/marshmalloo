About

Ri Liu is a designer who codes, and a coder who designs (she loves both equally as much). She has an interest in web technologies, interaction design and computational art.

She’s handy with a few languages (Ruby on Rails, PHP, C#, Processing & more) and her tools of choice include her mac, TextMate and Adobe CS4.

If you are interested in hiring Ri, or getting her on board for your next project, email her at ri@marshmalloo.com.

If you’re still not convinced, you should check our her resume.
